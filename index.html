What is TechNova?

TechNova Solutions is a growing Jordanian AI startup specializing in innovative and ethical technology solutions. As part of its commitment to professional development and social impact, TechNova was contracted by the National Innovation Hub (NIH) to design and deliver a training program titled “AI for Good: Ethics, Communication, and Collaboration.” This training program aims to support government organizations in building future-ready teams capable of working effectively in Smart City projects. The training is designed for 15 government analysts and engineers from NIH. It focuses on enhancing participants’ interpersonal and professional skills, particularly in communication, teamwork, ethical awareness in artificial intelligence, and effective time management under pressure. The program is delivered as a three-day training sprint spread across four weeks, ensuring flexibility while meeting project constraints. The main objectives of this training are to improve communication between working teams, promote a clear understanding of ethical considerations in AI, strengthen collaboration across departments, and enhance participants’ ability to prioritize tasks and manage time effectively. By combining structured planning, interactive learning activities, and professional communication practices, TechNova Solutions aims to deliver a high-impact training experience that supports both individual skill development and organizational performance.
